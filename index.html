Our Story

B. K. Charcoal has emerged as a trusted name in the charcoal manufacturing industry. Our state-of-the-art factory is strategically located in Parthibanur, Ramnathapuram district, enabling us to efficiently produce a wide range of high-quality charcoal products. Backed by a decade of experience, we take pride in manufacturing Grade Nalli, a premium charcoal known for its exceptional performance as a fuel in barbecue stoves.

At B. K. Charcoal, we go beyond conventional charcoal production. Our Root Charcoal is not only prized for tanduri cooking but also contains a unique blend of compounds, including calcium carbide, which provides a consistent and controlled release of heat. In addition, our state-of-the-art Ferrosilicon unit enhances the properties of our Average Sized Charcoal, making it a preferred choice for industrial applications requiring high temperatures and efficient combustion. These compounds ensure that our charcoal delivers exceptional performance, whether it's for cooking, industrial processes, or specialized applications like gold and lead melting.

In our commitment to cater to a wide array of requirements, we proudly manufacture Pakoda Charcoal. This versatile charcoal variety finds application in the production of sambrani and water purifiers, offering a reliable and efficient solution. With our advanced manufacturing techniques and strict quality control measures, we ensure that each batch of charcoal leaving our factory meets the highest standards of excellence

Our Products

GradeNalli

Grade Nalli is a high-quality charcoal known for its fine texture and extended surface area, making it easy to ignite. Its superior lighting properties reduce the overall lighting time, allowing grillers to start cooking faster. Additionally, Grade Nalli excels in holding temperatures for prolonged periods, making it ideal for low and slow BBQ chicken preparations. With its exceptional qualities, Grade Nalli is a top choice among grillers seeking consistent heat and prolonged cooking durations for a flavorful BBQ experience.

Root Charcoal

Root charcoal is a versatile fuel widely used in tandur ovens and as a power source for restaurant burners, including dosa tawa and other cooking equipment. Known for its high heat output and long-lasting burning properties, root charcoal provides the necessary energy for efficient and consistent cooking in professional kitchen settings. Its exceptional qualities make it a preferred choice among chefs and restaurant owners for achieving optimal results in their culinary endeavors.

Average Charcoal

Average-sized charcoal finds applications in energy production, pharmaceuticals, agriculture, and environmental remediation. It is used as a renewable fuel source, in medicine production, as a soil amendment, and for environmental cleanup. Its versatility makes it valuable across various industrial sectors.

Slim Sized Charcoal

Slim-sized charcoal is highly suitable for specific purposes such as hookah smoking, gold and lead melting, and other metal-related applications. Its compact shape and efficient burning characteristics make it ideal for generating the required heat and maintaining precise temperature control in these processes, ensuring optimal results.
